1

When I was a second-grade student in China, I didn't have access to any electrical devices except for television. "tv.jpeg"

2

When my classmate invited me to hang out at her house, I had no idea what a computer or laptop was. I was so confused when she suggested joining her for a multiplayer game on this website called 7k7k (a website similar to Coolmath), as that was the first interactive game or system that I had ever experienced. "fireboy and watergirl.mp3"

3

I still remember how challenging it was for me to use the WASD keys to control the character, but I had a lot of fun. Since then, my classmate and I hang out more often, and we became very close friends because we played games together and were in the same class. "computers in our daily life.pdf"

4

However, this happiness didn't last long. As the first semester of my third grade was coming to a close, I received the news that I would be returning to the U.S.A, back to the place where I was born. I was sad because I would miss my friend and our gaming experiences together.

5

The good thing is that I can still access 7k7k here with my own laptop in the U.S.A, but unfortunately, I can no longer join my friend in games anymore. "7k7k.mp4"
